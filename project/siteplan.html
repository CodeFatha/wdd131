TestDrive

The name is was chosen because it reflects the purpose of the website and is short and memorable too.

Purpose:

The site helps users book a testdrive based on the car they want and not have to worry about where to find the car or who the dealer is. They simply get on the websitee, scroll through the collection of cars and click on the image of the care they want to test drive or search it by name, fill in their information as well as the slot they want from the ones that are available and they will be redirected to dealerships near them who would also be notified and would have to reserve the slot for them.

Scenarios:

  • Where can I get a specific model and make of a car near me?
  • How can I book a test drive without navigating various sites?

Typography:

  • Sour Gummy (Headings and menu)
  • Roboto (paragragh)

Color Schema

  • Cadet Blue (header, footer and icons)
  • #393939 (Labels)
  • Dark grey and black (all other text)
